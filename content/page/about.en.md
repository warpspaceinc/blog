## About Warpspace Inc.

Warpspace Inc. is the company behind [Caveduck.io](https://caveduck.io), an innovative AI companion service designed to provide meaningful and engaging interactions.

### Our Mission

We believe in creating AI companions that are helpful, safe, and genuinely enjoyable to interact with. Our goal is to push the boundaries of what's possible in AI-human interaction while maintaining the highest standards of quality and user experience.

### Caveduck.io

Caveduck.io is our flagship product - an AI companion service that offers personalized, engaging conversations. Whether you're looking for a creative collaborator, a learning partner, or simply someone to chat with, Caveduck is here for you.
